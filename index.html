Crystal Collectors!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to the matching number, you will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden unitl you click on it.

Each time when the games starts, the game will change each value of the crystals.
